About Us

A Poway staple, built on community.

Papa's Liquor & Deli has been serving the Poway community with the same warmth and care since day one. Whether you're grabbing a sandwich for lunch, picking up a bottle for the weekend, or just stopping in to say hello — you're always welcome here.

We're a family-run shop that takes pride in knowing our regulars by name and making sure everyone who walks through our door leaves happy.
