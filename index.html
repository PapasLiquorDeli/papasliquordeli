Our Story

A neighborhood shop built for Poway.

Papa's Liquor & Deli is a proud family-owned business serving the Poway community with fresh food, quality products, and genuine hospitality. Since opening our doors, we have believed that every customer should feel like a neighbor, whether they are stopping in for a quick lunch, picking up a favorite bottle, or grabbing everyday essentials.

Our deli is built around fresh, made-to-order sandwiches, crisp salads, and ingredients we take pride in serving. Alongside our kitchen, you will find a carefully selected variety of beer, wine, spirits, snacks, and convenience items, making Papa's a dependable one-stop neighborhood destination.

More than anything, we are grateful for the relationships we have built with our customers over the years. Many familiar faces have become friends, and we are honored to continue serving the families, workers, students, and visitors who make our community so special.

Family Owned. Community Focused. Proudly Serving Poway.
