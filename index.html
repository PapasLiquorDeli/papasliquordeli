Our Story

A neighborhood shop built for Poway.

Papa's Liquor & Deli is a family-owned neighborhood store created with a simple goal: make every customer feel welcome and serve food we are proud to put our name on.

From regulars stopping in for their usual order to families preparing for a weekend gathering, we value the conversations, familiar faces, and relationships that make a local business feel like part of the community.

Every sandwich is made fresh and every visit matters. We are grateful to serve Poway, one order and one neighbor at a time.
